Misconceptions in advertising

After reading some of the concepts discussed in this article that relate to the field of advertising, you can rethink your company and advertising decisions. There are many ideas circulating that have nothing to do with health. Here are some of them:

A controversial ad is a good ad

Many people who work in advertising believe that a controversial ad is a good ad, and they make their decisions based on that. Being part of people's conversation is exciting, but such advertisements, although sometimes successful, may represent a risk that harms the company and affects its image with the public and its sales, so such advertisements must be taken seriously.

An advertising agency that only creates advertisements

The term “advertising agency” refers to an entity that provides many services, not limited to advertising only, but also including various consulting services, market research, strategy building, programming, design, and many others.

Brand identity is the logo

The logo is one of the elements of business identity, which is developed after many steps of study and planning. Brand identity is a story, a promise, an experience and a memory built over years and living in the minds of the public. It is worth noting that it can be planned, designed and modified if necessary.

There is no need to spend on marketing and advertising at the beginning of the business

Marketing and advertising are a necessary investment in business. Planning it and setting a budget, even if it is small, is an effective strategy for achieving the project goals, even if its return is sometimes long-term.
